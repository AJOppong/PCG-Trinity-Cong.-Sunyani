Children’s Service

“Train up a child in the way he should go” – Proverbs 22:6

“Let the children come to me, and do not stop them…”

Vision

That every child will grow up a Christian, in the home, community and Church, where his/her developmental needs are met in a holistic manner.

Mission

The Children’s Ministry works in partnership with others to bring children into the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ and nurture them to grow physically, intellectually, emotionally, socially, and spiritually into Christian maturity.

Objectives

  • That the unsaved child is won for Christ.
  • That the child develops his/her full potential in life.
  • That the saved child grows up into maturity and assurance of faith.
  • That the needs of needy children within and outside the Church are met.
  • That unsaved parents and guardians are reached for Christ.
  • That Christian parents are guided to raise their children in faith.
  • That the rights and needs of children are promoted in Church and society.
