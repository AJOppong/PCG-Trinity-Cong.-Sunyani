Church Choir

“Unifying Voices to Glorify God”

The Church Choir is at the heart of PCG Trinity’s hymns and chorale performances, enhancing worship with harmonious melodies and heartfelt expression.

History

Established in the early 2000s, the Church Choir has grown to perform traditional hymns, chorale pieces, and special songs for significant church events, bringing the congregation together in worship.

What We Do

  • Perform hymns and chorale songs during services.
  • Lead congregational singing for special events.
  • Train new members in vocal techniques and harmonization.
  • Support other church music groups in collaborative performances.

Departments Within Us

  • Soprano Section
  • Alto Section
  • Tenor Section
  • Bass Section
  • Accompaniment/Instrument Section
