Echoes of Trinity

"Enriching worship with contemporary harmonies and melodies."

History

Echoes of Trinity was formed in 2010 as a contemporary choir to bring a modern worship experience to PCG Trinity. The choir has grown to include over 40 members, blending traditional and modern music to enhance congregational worship.

What We Do

The choir leads worship during Sunday services, special church events, and annual celebrations. They blend contemporary, gospel, and traditional songs to create an immersive worship experience. The choir also conducts workshops and training sessions for new members to develop musical skills and spiritual growth.

Departments Within Echoes of Trinity

  • Vocal Team – Main singers and harmonizers.
  • Instrumental Team – Piano, guitar, drums, and percussion.
  • Audio & Sound Team – Manages sound and mixing for live services.
