About Us

Once upon a time in the year 2015, some young vibrant leaders of a group called Young Peoples’ Guild in the Presbyterian Church of Ghana, Brong Ahafo Presbytery, Sunyani District, in a Congregation in Tonsuom Estate- Suburb of Sunyani, known as Trinity Congregation, met in Prayer and God spoke to them about the three (3) dispensations; God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.

The team realized that in the era of God the father, they were not present and those that could not recognized the Son during His era too, they are not to blame but they’ve God all to do with the Holy Spirit in this generation. The team did not want to be in a dispensation and it be said about them that, ‘The Holy Spirit came to His own and His own received Him not’ and hence He should be recognized, received, and celebrated in the beauty of his Holi- ness and his works for the world knows Him not but we know Him. (John 14:17)

This caused the team to start a festival of the Spirit for the World to get to know Him, for Christians to accept His works and for the Chosen to celebrate Him in the program called “HOLY GHOST FESTIVAL”.

The first program was held in 3rd to 9th August 2015, with the late Rev. J. O. Ahinakwah as the host minister. The Spirit led us to strategize for the program never had been in the Presbyterian Church before in what was seen as ‘One night, One Minister’ where we paraded the teachers, Prophets, Apostles in the Church, not only Presbyterian Ministers but all ministers of the Word in the Church of Christ and it has been so till date.

Mission & Vision

Our purpose stems from the Mission and Vision of PCG but by extension, we seek to

  • Outdoor the Holy Spirit,
  • Place emphasis on His Works
  • Bring the Body of Christ under the same umbrella,
  • Celebrate Him for who He is and
  • Revive the Church of God
  • In so doing, people will come to know Christ, and the gifts and fruit of the Spirit will be made mani- fest in their lives.

    • This is re-living the upper room experience
