About Us

Presbyterian Church of Ghana, Trinity Congregation, is a Christ-centered community devoted to worship, fellowship, and service. Guided by our motto—“Trinity, we are a family of God; Trinity, we are blessed; Trinity, we are untouchable”—we seek to nurture individuals and families in faith, drawing them closer to God through prayer, teaching, and love.

As a family of believers, we uphold the values of unity, compassion, and holiness, striving to make disciples who live by God’s Word and share His light with the world. At Trinity, we walk together in faith, encourage one another in hope, and celebrate the abundant blessings of God’s grace...

About Scholarships

Our scholarships are designed to support members in education, leadership, and community service. We aim to nurture talent, promote academic excellence, and strengthen the faith of our congregation.

History & Purpose

Established in 2010, the scholarship program has awarded over 200 students, helping them pursue academic and spiritual growth. Its purpose is to foster educational excellence and community engagement among members.

Administrative Structure

The scholarship program is managed by a dedicated committee under the church's board of education. Members of the committee oversee applications, review criteria, and ensure transparency in awarding scholarships.

Eligibility

Applicants must be active members of the congregation, demonstrate academic potential, and be engaged in church or community activities. Specific criteria vary by scholarship type.
