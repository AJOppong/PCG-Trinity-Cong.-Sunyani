Brigade

“Order and Discipline in Service to God”

The Brigade is responsible for maintaining order and discipline during church services and events, ensuring smooth flow and assisting members as needed.

History

Founded alongside the church’s early ministries, the Brigade has provided consistent support in organizing services, coordinating events, and maintaining decorum for over a decade.

What We Do

  • Manage seating and ushering during church services.
  • Ensure smooth flow of events and activities.
  • Support special programs and large gatherings.
  • Train members in leadership, discipline, and teamwork.

Departments Within Us

  • Ushering Unit
  • Event Coordination Team
  • Training & Development Team
