About Men's Fellowship

Men’s Fellowship is an active generational group for all men between the ages of 40 and above. The group was started in 2019. Currently, the membership strength of the group is about 12.

Goals

  • Foster strong, healthy and brotherly relationships amongst members.
  • Help meet the spiritual and material needs of members.
  • Rally round and support members in times of hardships and celebrations.
  • Encourage all men in Hope Congregation to join the Men’s Fellowship.
