Singing Band

“Leading Worship Through Music and Praise”

The Singing Band has been the heartbeat of PCG Trinity’s traditional music ministry, creating a rich tapestry of melodies that enrich our worship experience.

History

Founded in the early 2000s, the Singing Band has grown from a small group of dedicated musicians into a full ensemble that leads traditional worship music across all church services and special events.

What We Do

  • Lead congregational singing during church services.
  • Perform special traditional music during festivals and celebrations.
  • Train new members in instruments and vocal techniques.
  • Collaborate with other church groups for special programs.
