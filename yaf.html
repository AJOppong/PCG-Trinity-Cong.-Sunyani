About YAF

Young Adults Fellowship (YAF) is an active generational group for all young adults of the congregation between the ages of 30 and 40. The group was started in 2019. Currently, YAF stands with a membership strength of about 12 registered members who meet once a month to fellowship through various activities, corporate prayer, and community involvement to enhance holistic growth of our members.

Aims & Objectives

To address issues permeating in the spiritual, economical, technological, and social lives of young adults to equip and empower them for the benefit of the church and community/state.
