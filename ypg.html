Young People's Guild (YPG)

"To know His will and to do it"

About YPG

The Young People's Guild (YPG) is made up of persons within the ages of eighteen to thirty years. The Holy Hill Congregation YPG started in 2020 with a total of ten (10) members.

Leadership

  • Paul Abisa – President

Meeting Days

The group meets usually on the second or third Sunday of the month.

Programme & Activities

Activities of the group cut across several themes concentrating on spiritual, social, psychological, academic, emotional, and personal development of the individual. Most of the programs are held online, but periodically the members meet in person to fellowship together.

Gallery

How to Join

Anyone within the ages of eighteen to thirty is welcome to join the group. That person must make the commitment to participate in the programs and activities of the group.
